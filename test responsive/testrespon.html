Rangikohu Marae

Te Haukainga Hall Rangikohu was built in the mid 1900s on the Homestead of Iwa Adams nee Hapakuku and husband Toma Adams. Then in the 1990s another Marae named Rangikohu Marae was built on the corner of Epikauri and Rangikohu Roads, which is situated North across from Owhata/ Herekino Harbour, it represents the hapū of Ngāti Kuri rāua ko Ngāti Wairupe and Ki Whangape, and also Te Aupōuri.
